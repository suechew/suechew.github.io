L'Homme

Yves Saint Laurent

L'Homme by Yves Saint Laurent is a woody yet floral fragrance with an overall musky body.

It has top notes of ginger, bergamot, and lemon;

Middle notes of spice, white pepper, basil and violet leaf;

Base notes of iris, cedar, and tonka bean.
